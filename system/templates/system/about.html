About this game

A game created suddenly.

Profile

You can change your profile by replacing body parts (6 parts) at will.

Rules

The game is divided into 2 phases:
in the first phase you will simply have to crush the yellow squares that are shown on the screen. If you push it before it disappears you'll score a point!
(You can see your score on the top right)
Meanwhile, other players will be playing like you and if someone crushes a yellow square in the same position where you crushed it too, the battle will start!
(In the picture your profile is on the left and your opponent's on the right)
